About Spectrum
Since last 11 years, PCCOE SPECTRUM has been a major state level event for first year students. The thing that makes SPECTRUM really special is the fact that the entire event is managed, created and participated in by EXCLUSIVELY first year students.
The major objective of this event is to provide a platform exclusively to First Year Engineering students to express and apply their innovative ideas and hidden talents in the competitive technological scenario.
A State Level technical competition which included nine different events. It is a mega carnival where young minds with innovative thought come under one roof to showcase their skills.
